# GradLedger 0.5.0

GradLedger 0.5.0 is the first student-ready ZIP release.

Highlights:

- Capture professor, lab, and graduate-program pages across a same-site browsing session.
- Extract names, universities, countries, research interests, contact instructions, emails, and recruiting evidence.
- Save individual reports locally and export one or many reports as CSV.
- Use deterministic extraction rules with evidence and confidence instead of an external AI service.
- Install manually in Chrome from `gradledger-0.5.0.zip`.

Known limitations:

- Unusual layouts, image-only text, PDFs, login pages, and blocked or heavily dynamic pages may be incomplete.
- Open-position status remains unknown when a page does not make an explicit recruiting statement.
- Review extracted fields against the official university website before contacting a professor or applying.
